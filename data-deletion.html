Data Deletion Request

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

Your Right to Data Deletion

At BitLogic, we respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information that we collect and maintain.

What Data Can Be Deleted

You can request deletion of the following types of data:

  • Account information and profile data
  • Email address and contact information
  • Usage data and analytics associated with your account
  • Support tickets and communication history
  • Any other personal information you have provided to us

Data We Cannot Delete

Certain data may be retained for legitimate business purposes or legal requirements:

  • Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be linked to you
  • Data required for fraud prevention or security purposes
  • Information required to comply with legal obligations
  • Data that has been shared with third parties and is outside our control

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal data, please follow these steps:

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to Support@bitlogic.info with the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your BitLogic account
  • Specific data you want deleted (or "all personal data" for complete deletion)
  • Confirmation that you are the account owner

Option 2: In-App Request

If you have the BitLogic app installed:

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Data Deletion Request
  • Follow the prompts to submit your request
  • You will receive a confirmation email

Verification Process

To protect your privacy and prevent unauthorized data deletion, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request. This may include:

  • Confirming your email address
  • Requesting additional identification if needed
  • Verifying you are the account owner

Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receipt and verification. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted or if we need additional information.

What Happens After Deletion

Once your data is deleted:

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • You will lose access to all BitLogic services
  • Your personal data will be removed from our active systems
  • Some data may remain in backup systems for a limited time

Data Retention Exceptions

We may retain certain information after your deletion request:

  • For legal compliance and regulatory requirements
  • To resolve disputes or enforce our agreements
  • For fraud detection and prevention
  • To maintain security and integrity of our systems

Third-Party Data

If you have linked third-party services or shared data with external platforms, you will need to contact those services directly to request data deletion from their systems.

Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw your consent for data processing at any time. However, this may affect your ability to use certain features of our app.

Contact Information

For questions about data deletion or to submit a request, please contact us:

Email: Support@bitlogic.info

Website: bitlogic.info

Appeals Process

If you believe we have not properly handled your data deletion request, you can:

  • Reply to our response email explaining your concerns
  • Request a review by our privacy team
  • Contact relevant data protection authorities in your jurisdiction

Legal Framework

This data deletion policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store Data Deletion Requirements
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
