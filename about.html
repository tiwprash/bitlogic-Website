About BitLogic

Empowering Traders with Zero-Code Automation

Who We Are

BitLogic was founded with a singular mission: to democratize algorithmic trading. For years, the ability to rapidly scan the crypto markets for complex technical conditions and build automated trading strategies was locked behind a barrier of programming knowledge. We saw traders struggling to monitor multiple timeframes across hundreds of coins simultaneously, missing out on critical opportunities.

Our team of experienced software engineers, data scientists, and veteran crypto traders came together to build a solution. The result is BitLogic—a state-of-the-art, no-code crypto strategy builder and real-time market screener designed to give everyday traders the same analytical firepower as institutional quants.

Our Mission

We believe that technology should serve the trader, not complicate their workflow. We strive to provide unparalleled access to advanced technical indicators, backtesting paradigms, and real-time scanning through an intuitive, visual interface. Whether you're tracking a simple RSI divergence or a complex Bollinger Band squeeze combined with MACD crossovers, BitLogic does the heavy lifting so you can focus on strategy.

Why Trust Us?

Transparency and reliability are the cornerstones of BitLogic. Our scanning engines connect directly to top-tier exchanges like Binance, Bybit, and CoinDCX to ensure you receive the most accurate and up-to-date market data. We are continuously refining our algorithms to minimize latency, ensuring that when your strategy's conditions are met, you receive a push notification instantly.

We do not manage your funds, ask for your private keys, or execute trades automatically on your behalf without your explicit permission and secure API integration. BitLogic is, at its core, an analytical tool designed to enhance your decision-making.
